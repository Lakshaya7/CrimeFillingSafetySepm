About the Project

The Crime Reporting and Safety Management System (CRSMS) is a digital platform designed to simplify the reporting and investigation of criminal activities. It allows citizens to submit complaints online, track case progress, and access emergency services. Law enforcement agencies gain an integrated dashboard for case assignment, evidence tracking, communication, and report generation.

The system reduces paperwork, increases transparency, accelerates investigations, and provides a safer environment through data-driven policing. It plays a crucial role in enhancing accountability and improving the cooperation between citizens and law enforcement.
